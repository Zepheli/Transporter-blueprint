Transporter

A mixture of magic and science.
A machine that able to transport living and non living things by deconstructing them in one place and reconstructing them on different remote place.

This machine will work in pairs of course. The connection will be made with magical runes.



This will be powered by magmatic power. The heat of the magma will turn the water, that will be put in a tube made out of a mythril and nyeshk alloy, into steams that will activate the turbine. The turbine will power the generator that will be conecter to the base of the transporter with orichalcum wiring.





The energy will be stored on a power bank. The power bank will be made with a tyrium and mythril alloy with some crystals inside.
